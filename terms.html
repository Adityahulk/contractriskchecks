Terms & Data Handling

Last updated: February 2026

These terms describe how pilot engagements work.

Scope of service

  • The engagement is a time-boxed pilot, not a long-term software subscription.
  • Typical pilot duration is 10-14 days, with limited scope.

Nature of access

  • All access provided is read-only.
  • No systems are modified.
  • No automated actions are taken.
  • No workflows are changed.

What this service provides

  • Identification of potential contract and renewal risks where contract terms and CRM state may not align.
  • A written report outlining what may have slipped, why it matters, and suggested next steps.

What this service does not provide

  • This is not legal advice.
  • This is not a billing audit.
  • This is not a compliance certification.
  • Findings are informational and advisory in nature.

Responsibility & ownership

  • The customer retains full ownership and control of all systems and data.
  • Any decisions or actions taken based on findings remain the customer’s responsibility.

Termination

  • The pilot can be stopped at any time by either party.
  • Access will be revoked immediately upon termination.

These terms are intended to keep the pilot lightweight, safe, and focused on visibility rather than control.
